The most common questions

What is your experience in managing social media?


We have extensive experience in managing social media for companies across various sectors, including technology, fashion, food, and healthcare. Our team consists of experts who have successfully managed campaigns that increased engagement and conversions.

Which social media platforms do you manage?


We manage all major social media platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and TikTok. We tailor our strategy for each platform, ensuring that your brand’s message is effectively communicated across all channels.

What is your approach to creating a social media strategy?


We start with a detailed analysis of your brand, target audience, and competitors. Then, we develop a customized strategy that includes clear objectives, content calendars, engagement plans, and growth tactics. We continuously monitor and adjust the strategy to ensure we are achieving the best possible results.

How do you measure the success of a social media campaign?


We measure the success of a campaign using various KPIs, such as engagement rate, reach, impressions, follower growth, clicks, conversions, and ROI. We provide detailed reports regularly to keep you informed about your campaigns’ performance.

Do you offer content creation services?


Yes, we offer comprehensive content creation services, including graphic design, video production, copywriting, and photography. Our goal is to create visually appealing and relevant content that resonates with your audience and promotes your brand.

What posting frequency do you recommend?


The ideal posting frequency can vary depending on the platform and target audience. Generally, we recommend posting on Instagram and Facebook at least 3-5 times a week, on Twitter multiple times a day, and on LinkedIn 1-2 times a week. We adjust the frequency based on engagement and follower feedback.

How do you handle negative feedback and comments?


We handle negative feedback and comments professionally and promptly. We continuously monitor social media to respond quickly and resolve any issues. We work to turn negative experiences into opportunities to demonstrate the brand’s commitment to customer satisfaction.

What sets Mixar Digital Studio apart from other digital agencies?


Our team comprises talented individuals who are not only skilled in their respective fields but also have a deep passion for innovation. We strive to deliver unique, captivating, and highly customized digital solutions that surpass expectations.
